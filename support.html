Report content or a user

Everyone can report or block within the app. On any post, tap the ⋯ menu and choose Report post or Block user; on a profile, tap the flag icon to report or block. Reports are reviewed, and abusive content or accounts are removed. You can also email us at the address above to report something.

Common questions

  • Delete my account. Profile → gear (top‑right) → Delete account. This removes your account and everything in it.
  • Turn off notifications. Profile → gear → Notifications, or your device’s iOS Settings.
  • Forgot your password. On the sign‑in screen, tap “Forgot password?” and follow the emailed link.
  • Privacy. See our Privacy Policy.
